Life Below Water

The ocean is a big part of our world. It gives us food, air, and life. But in recent years, the ocean is being negatively impacted because of what people do. This page dedicates to helps everyone understand why "Life Below Water" is important and why we must protect our oceans.
Learn More

The Problem

Oceans face pollution, overfishing, coral bleaching, and habitat loss. Many species are threatened and coastal communities are affected.

Pollution

Plastic and chemical pollution harms marine life and can enter the food chain.

Overfishing

Unsustainable fishing removes too many fish, upsetting ecosystem balance.

Urgent: coastal and marine ecosystems are declining. Immediate action can still make a big difference.

Solutions

Actions that help restore and protect marine ecosystems.

Reduce Plastic

Cut single-use plastics, improve waste management, and support cleanup efforts.

Sustainable Fishing

Adopt sustainable quotas, protect breeding grounds, and support local stewardship.

Marine Protected Areas

Designate protected zones where ecosystems can recover naturally.

Community-led projects and policy changes both matter — they scale when combined.
